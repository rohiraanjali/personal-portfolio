Richard Davis

Richard Davies is the CEO and founder of Evbex, the estate and facilities management support organisation, Evbex Asia and Evbex-FRP.

He has written various papers and publications, and is a visiting lecturer at various universities, providing training and sharing his insights. He represents UK in the International Standards Organisation(ISO), contributing towards the new breed of International FM standards, including the acclaimed ISO 41001: Facilities Management Systems. Further, he also contributed towards FM and Maintenance Standards within British Standards Institution(BSI).
